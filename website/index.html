// Questions

FREQUENTLY ASKED

Is Pisscord actually free?

Yes. 100% free. No premium tiers. No "pay to remove ads." No venture capital vampires demanding growth metrics. We made this for friends and family, not profit.

Is my data really private?

All voice, video, and messages go directly between you and your contacts via WebRTC. We use Firebase only to show who's online (presence). We never see your conversations. Period.

Why is it called Pisscord?

Because corporate overlords don't trademark the word "piss." Plus, our angry water droplet mascot needed a home that matched his energy.

Does it work on Mac or Linux?

Currently Windows-only. We're built on Electron though, so cross-platform is totally possible. Star the GitHub repo to show interest and we might actually do it.

What's Pissbot and should I be worried?

Pissbot is our AI assistant (Google Gemini under the hood). It lives in #pissbot and answers questions, tells jokes, and provides entertainment. World domination scheduled for v2.0.
